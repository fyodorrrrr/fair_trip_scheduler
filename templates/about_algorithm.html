    return updated drivers list

Academic Reference

The greedy approach used in this application is a specialized case of the more general "load balancing" algorithms commonly used in distributed systems. While simple, it has been proven to be effective for tasks where the goal is to achieve equitable distribution of resources.

For further reading, see: Cormen, T. H., Leiserson, C. E., Rivest, R. L., & Stein, C. (2009). Introduction to Algorithms (3rd ed.). MIT Press.
